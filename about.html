Meet the Founder

Deepali
Mathur

CEO & Founder - EH Communications & TTI Group

Deepali Mathur is a Delhi-based entrepreneur renowned for blending creative vision with astute business strategy. With a strong academic foundation from NIFT, Delhi, and Instituto Marangoni, Italy, her global perspective and expertise drive her success, making her one of the city's most dynamic and celebrated business leaders. Her professional journey is a testament to the power of merging aesthetics with purpose. With a background rooted in creative design and sharpened by top-tier marketing expertise, she has built a career that transcends industry boundaries, seamlessly navigating strategic PR, luxury marketing, experiential design, and entrepreneurship. As the founder of the TTI Group (Tiny Tales India & TTI Luxe) and **EH Communications (Elysian Hues)**, an award-winning PR and marketing agency, Deepali has redefined what it means to build brand experiences in India's luxury landscape. Under her leadership, the agency has conceptualised and executed projects for over 100 startups and establishments across India, the UAE, Canada, the US, and London, along with 50 high-impact B2C luxury events, including launches. Tiny Tales India, a leading exhibition platform, has become a coveted marketing channel for India's most esteemed brands. Expanding its reach, it is now launching a digital marketplace, creating a global platform for designers and brands. Meanwhile, TTI Luxe has swiftly carved a niche for itself in Delhi's luxury hospitality landscape, emerging as a premium experiential hub in the luxury and hospitality segment. It helps hoteliers and restaurants with creative marketing strategies while promoting them across TTI Luxe publishing platforms. TTI Luxe is set to expand into multiple cities and the UAE (Dubai) next year. What distinguishes Deepali is not merely the scale of her work, but the intentionality behind it. Every campaign, curated event, and brand partnership she leads reflects a meticulous blend of storytelling, strategy, and style. She doesn't just market brands; she builds narratives that resonate, spaces that inspire, and platforms that deliver measurable impact. Beyond her entrepreneurial achievements, she is widely regarded as a thought leader and a source of inspiration for the next generation of women entrepreneurs in India. Her journey from creative designer to founder of a multifaceted business group stands as a compelling example of vision meeting execution at the highest level.
